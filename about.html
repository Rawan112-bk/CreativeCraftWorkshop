Who We Are

Creative Craft Workshop is a website designed to help users explore creative activities such as candle making, pottery, and painting. Our goal is to make it easy for visitors to discover workshops, learn more about each activity, and register online.

This project was developed as part of the Web Development course. We focused on creating a clean, user-friendly, and responsive website using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
